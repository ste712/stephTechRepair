About Stephen Tech Repairs

Stephen Tech Repairs was founded by SSEMAMBO STEPHEN, a student at St Lawrence University with a passion for IT and a commitment to quality technical service. We understand how essential your mobile phone is, whether for school, business, or connecting with family. That's why we pride ourselves on offering free, transparent diagnostics and backing all our core services with a clear warranty.
